Tremolo
Uses amplification to create a shuddering effect, where the volume quickly oscillates.
$tremolo 1 50

Example:

$tremolo 1 50 - Sets the tremolo settings to 1Hz, covering 50% of the volume.

Explenation:

The first argument of this command is how many times a second the volume should change and the second argument is how much it needs to change, ranging from 1% to 100%

Required permissions:

A role named "DJ", Manage Messages permission or being alone with the bot.
Vibrato
Uses amplification to create a shuddering effect, where the pitch quickly oscillates.
$vibrato 1 50

Example:

$vibrato 1 50 - Sets the tremolo settings to target band 0, covering 50%.

Explenation:

Similar to tremolo. While tremolo oscillates the volume, vibrato oscillates the pitch. The first argument of this command is what band it targets, ranging from 0 (low frequency) to 14 (high frequency) and the second argument is how much it needs to change, ranging from 1% to 100%

Required permissions:

A role named "DJ", Manage Messages permission or being alone with the bot.
